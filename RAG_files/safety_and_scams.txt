Common travel scams: Unofficial taxis at airports. "Broken" taxi meters. Distraction theft (someone spills something on you while an accomplice steals your bag). Overly friendly locals offering unsolicited help. Fake police asking to see your wallet.

Safety basics: Keep valuables in front pockets or hidden pouches. Don't display expensive jewelry or electronics unnecessarily. Stay aware in crowded tourist areas. Trust your instincts.

Digital safety: Use VPNs on public WiFi. Enable two-factor authentication. Keep backup copies of important documents in cloud storage.
