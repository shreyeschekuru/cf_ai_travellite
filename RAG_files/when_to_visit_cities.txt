Peak season: Best weather, longest hours for attractions, most events and activities. Highest prices, largest crowds, advance booking essential.

Shoulder season: Moderate weather, fewer crowds, better prices. Most attractions still open. Best balance for most travelers.

Off-season: Lowest prices, smallest crowds, authentic local experience. Some attractions closed or limited hours. Weather may be challenging. Great for budget travelers and return visitors.

Local events and holidays: Research major festivals and holidays. Can be amazing experiences or create closures and crowds. Book far in advance if visiting during major events.
