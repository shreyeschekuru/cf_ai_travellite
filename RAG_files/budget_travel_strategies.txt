Budget tier (under $50/day): Stay in hostels or budget hotels outside city centers. Use public transit exclusively. Eat at local markets and street food vendors. Focus on free attractions like parks, walking tours, and museums with free days.

Mid-range tier ($50-150/day): Mix of mid-range hotels and Airbnbs in good neighborhoods. Combine public transit with occasional ride-shares. Balance casual dining with a few nicer restaurant experiences. Paid attractions and tours are feasible.

Luxury tier ($150+/day): Hotels in prime locations. Private transport or ride-shares as needed. Fine dining and premium experiences. VIP tours and skip-the-line access to major attractions.
