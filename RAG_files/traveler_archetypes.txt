Adventure seekers: Prioritize outdoor activities, hiking, water sports, and physical experiences. Early starts, packed days. Prefer hostels or budget accommodations to save money for activities.

Culture enthusiasts: Museums, historical sites, architecture, local arts. Moderate pace with deep dives into specific interests. Value knowledgeable guides and audio tours.

Food lovers: Restaurant reservations, food tours, cooking classes, local markets. Plan itinerary around meals. Willing to travel to specific neighborhoods for authentic cuisine.

Nightlife enthusiasts: Late starts, focus on evening and night activities. Live music, bars, clubs, late-night food scenes. Hotels in walkable nightlife districts.

Relaxation seekers: Slow pace, cafes, parks, spa experiences. Avoid over-scheduling. Prefer quiet neighborhoods and accommodations with good amenities.
